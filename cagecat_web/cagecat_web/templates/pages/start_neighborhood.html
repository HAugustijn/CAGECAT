Get started with geneNeighborhood

Explore the genomic context of your genes, EFI-GNT style. Give it a query (or a finished cblaster search) and it fetches the genes surrounding every hit from NCBI, then draws each locus to scale, aligned on its query gene, with homologous genes sharing a colour.

  • From a cblaster job
  • Search sequences
  • Results for existing job
